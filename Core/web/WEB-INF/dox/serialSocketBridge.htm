Mango Serial Socket Bridge

The Serial Socket Bridge is a type of virtual serial port that allows Mango to transmit/receive serial data over a TCP/IP network. The bridge acts as a client that will connect to an open TCP/IP port on a host. The bridge will show up in the Comm port listings for applicable data sources. By setting the host and port Mango will attempt to open up a TCP/IP socket and transmit data and receive messages. The timeout setting is referred to as the SO_TIMEOUT and is the timeout in milliseconds that Mango will wait during read operations. The timeout cannot be less than 0 and a timeout of 0 is interpreted as an infinite timeout.
